The uploaded billionaire (from Reamde) who becomes the demiurge of Bitworld under his old gamer handle.
Sophia
Dodge's grandniece, who first reanimates his connectome — then enters the digital world herself.

'C-plus' — Dodge's friend, executor, and steward of the project in meatspace.

The recurring ageless figure, again threading Stephenson's universe.
